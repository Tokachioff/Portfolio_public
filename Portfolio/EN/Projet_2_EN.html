Alten Site

In my first year at BUT Informatique, I participated in a group project to reproduce the website of Alten, but adapting it for a public of college students. The objective was to make the content more accessible and understandable for a young audience while maintaining the structure and slightly modifying the visual identity of the original site. This project allowed us to apply our web development skills using HTML, CSS and JavaScript languages to design a dynamic and intuitive interface.

Within our group of four students, I was responsible for the development of the "Sectors of Activity" page. I worked on the organization of content, layout and interactivity to make navigation smooth and enjoyable for users. I have integrated menus in JavaScript and optimized the design in CSS to improve the user experience. This work allowed me to strengthen my technical skills, especially in HTML structuring and (native) JavaScript manipulation.

In addition to my main mission, I also indirectly took on the role of project manager. I coordinated the various tasks between team members, ensuring that deadlines were met and the site as a whole was consistent. I also facilitated communication and decision-making to ensure a good division of labour and ensure a homogeneous final rendering. This experience has given me a better understanding of teamwork and project management responsibilities.

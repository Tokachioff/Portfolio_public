Aqua Athletics

At the end of our first year of a BUT (Computer Science) program, we worked as a team to create a website (in English) simulating that of a company offering water sports activities on a lake. The goal was to design an attractive and functional platform that would allow users to view the activities on offer, obtain practical information, and possibly simulate a reservation. This project allowed us to explore the various stages of website design, from initial mockup to final development.

This project drew on several skills acquired over the year, including web development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript), project management, and collaborative work. One of the main objectives was to effectively structure and distribute tasks within the team, while ensuring visual and functional consistency across the site. This also allowed us to put into practice concepts of responsive design and accessibility.

We used tools like Visual Studio Code for development, Github for version control, and Whimsical for mockup creation. The project allowed us to deepen our understanding of web languages, learn how to organize a project with others, and better understand the challenges of a clear and intuitive user interface. This teamwork also helped us improve our communication and discover effective methods for collaborating in a technical context.

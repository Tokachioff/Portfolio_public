Accident Study

At the end of my first year of a BUT in Computer Science, I carried out a project on my own to analyze data on road accidents among young people aged 18 to 25, in the Provence-Alpes-CÃ´te d'Azur region, between 2006 and 2023. The work consisted of using government Open Data to produce a synthetic and informative document.

The project allowed me to leverage my skills in data processing, statistical analysis, and visualization. The goal was to identify trends in accidents involving young people by cross-referencing several variables such as severity, causes, and traffic conditions. Working alone helped me gain autonomy and rigor.

I first used SQL to extract and clean data from the database. Then, I worked with RStudio to analyze the data and produce clear and relevant graphs. This project allowed me to strengthen my skills in data manipulation, statistical analysis, and visual communication of results.
